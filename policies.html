Returns & Refunds

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Return Window

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for standard items. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with all tags attached.

How to Return

To initiate a return, log into your account and navigate to your order history. Select the item(s) you wish to return and follow the prompts. You will receive a prepaid return label via email within 24 hours.

Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund within 5–7 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Custom and personalized orders
  • Sale items marked as final sale
  • Underwear and swimwear for hygiene reasons
  • Items that have been worn, washed, or altered

Exchanges

We offer free exchanges for size or color within 30 days of delivery. To exchange an item, follow the same process as a return and select "Exchange" instead of "Refund."

Custom Orders Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Custom Order Process

Custom orders are created through our BLVKOUT Studio. You can select any garment from our collection and customize it with your own print, a print from our library, or a design you upload.

Print Methods

DTG (Direct to Garment): Best for complex, multi-color designs. Printed directly onto the fabric for a soft feel. Recommended for 100% cotton garments.

DTF (Direct to Film): Best for vibrant colors and fine details. Applied as a heat transfer film. Works on all fabric types including blends.

File Requirements

  • Minimum resolution: 300 DPI
  • Accepted formats: PNG (preferred), PDF, AI, PSD
  • Maximum file size: 50MB
  • Color mode: RGB for DTG, CMYK for DTF

Production Timeline

Custom orders require 7–14 business days for production. Rush production (3–5 days) is available for an additional fee of $25.00.

Cancellation Policy

Custom orders may be cancelled within 24 hours of placement for a full refund. After 24 hours, cancellations are not accepted as production may have already begun.

Quality Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of our custom prints. If your order arrives with a print defect or error on our part, we will reprint or refund at no cost to you.

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website. We use this data to improve our services.

Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services such as Google Analytics and Meta Pixel that may set their own cookies. These are governed by the respective third parties' privacy policies.
